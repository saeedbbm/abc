HOW-TO PATTERN SPEC — VOLUNTEER HOURS TRACKING
================================================================================

Project:      Volunteer Hours Tracking
Origin:       Slack #engineering 2025-04-14 — Matt says "Jack has a PR coming
              for the volunteer hours tracking API." Customer feedback #6
              (Mike Nguyen, 2025-08-14) asks for downloadable volunteer hours

This feature extends the completed Volunteer Portal (PAW-20, PAW-21). It adds
hours tracking and export. The how-to should reference patterns from the
volunteer portal implementation as precedent.

================================================================================

API-1     Response envelope { data, meta }
          What to check: new hours tracking endpoints use the standard
          response wrapper middleware.

API-2     No pagination for small lists (under ~20 items)
          What to check: a volunteer's hours log for a single shelter is
          likely small enough to load in full. References Matt's pattern
          and the volunteer portal precedent (PR #52, #55).

          What to check: new endpoints go under /api/v1/volunteers/hours
          or similar.

NAV-1     Vertical left sidebar for selection
          What to check: if there is a UI for selecting a shelter or date
          range, uses vertical sidebar pattern per Tim's convention.

NAV-6     Single column for forms
          What to check: any data-entry forms (manual hour entry) use
          single-column layout per Tim.

PROC-1    Matt reviews backend, Tim reviews frontend
          What to check: Matt reviews Jack's backend PR.
